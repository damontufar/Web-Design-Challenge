Summary: Latitude vs. X

The purpose of this project was to analyze how weather's cities are affected by their closeness with the equator. The methodology followed included te extraction of random coordinates from the OpenWeatherMap API in order to analyze the data of 500 cities.

Once having the results, Matplotlib library was used to plot different weather measures with the objective of finding insights from the relationships between weather and latitude, this measures included: temperature, humidity, cloudiness and wind speed.

The purpose of this site is to provide the resulting data and visualizations from the analysis explained above, as well as the final results from the relationships that were founded.
